<p><i>DroidPlay</i> is an Android client for Apple's AirPlay (version 1). Its features include:</p><ul><li>Automatic discovery of AirPlay receivers within the same network</li><li>Beam photos from your device to AirPlay</li><li>Stream videos and music from your device to AirPlay</li><li>Mirror the screen from your device to AirPlay (only available on Android 5.0 and higher)</li></ul><p>Instructions can be found with the project's Readme.</p>
